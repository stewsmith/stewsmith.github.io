This Checklist is for a 24 or 48 hour hackathon

__Technology__
-Laptop & Charger
-Phone & Charger
-Hardware related to Hack

__Clothing__
-One extra T-shirt (plenty to be given out)
-3 Boxers
-5 pairs of socks
-Pants you are wearing now
-Sweatshirt
-Slippers

__Hygiene__
-Deodorant
-Soap for washing face
-Hand towel
-Toothbrush & toothpaste
-Floss

__Sleeping__
-Sleeping Pad
-Blanket
-Pillow

__Miscellaneous__
-Bouncy Ball
